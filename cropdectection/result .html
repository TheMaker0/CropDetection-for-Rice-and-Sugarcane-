Disease Detected: Brownspot (Rice)

Mild
Confidence Scores:
Brownspot (Rice): 100.0%
Disease Information:
Type: Fungal Disease ℹ️
Symptoms:
Small, reddish-brown circular or oval spots on leaves
Spots may merge, leading to large, necrotic (dead) patches
Premature leaf drying and reduced photosynthesis
Possible Causes:
High humidity and prolonged leaf wetness ℹ️
Infection spreads through windborne spores and contaminated plant debris ℹ️
Poor field sanitation and overuse of nitrogen fertilizers ℹ️
Management Strategies:
Use disease-resistant rice varieties
Apply appropriate fungicides when necessary
Maintain proper field sanitation and remove infected debris
Avoid excessive nitrogen fertilizer application
Ensure proper spacing for good air circulation
